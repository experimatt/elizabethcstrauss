What is the Feldenkrais Method®?
The Feldenkrais Method® of somatic education, i.e., relating to the body, uses gentle movement and directed attention to help people become more aware of how they are moving and learn new and more effective ways to function. You will be guided with gentle touch and/or verbal direction to become more aware of habits and tensions, develop a clearer sense of skeletal support, and explore new possibilities.

The Feldenkrais Method® was developed by Dr. Moshe Feldenkrais, physicist, mechanical engineer, and martial artist, based on principles of physics, biomechanics, learning, and human development.

“Make the impossible possible, the possible easy, and the easy elegant.”
— Moshe Feldenkrais, PhD
